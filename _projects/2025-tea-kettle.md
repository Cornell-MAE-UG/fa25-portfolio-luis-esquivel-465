## Project Overview

- Modeled heating as a transient lumped-capacitance energy balance  
- Calculated theoretical vs actual efficiency  
- Determined the effective thermal resistance and heat loss  
- Compared electrical work input to enthalpy rise of water  
- Evaluated steady-state vs transient behavior  
- Conducted experimental validation with temperature vs time data
